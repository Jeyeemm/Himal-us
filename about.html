We are researchers and undergraduates from West Visayas State University under the College of Informations and Communications Technology taking the course of BSEMC or Bachealor of Science and Entertainment and Multimedia Computing. We are aiming to preserve and publicize the culture of Guimaras by creating a digital comic adaptation of the story of Himal us, specifically focusing on the story of the two lovers Guima and Aras. In doing so, we also aim to enhance our abilities to the point that our work gets recognized and our names are known in the industry.
