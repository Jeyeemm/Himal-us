Our Mission

At Himal-us, our mission is to celebrate and preserve the cultural legacy of Guimaras through the artistry of multimedia storytelling. We aspire to bridge the gap between tradition and technology, bringing to life the island's rich story in a visually captivating and accessible manner. Through our comics and digital exploration, we aim to not only entertain but also educate and inspire an appreciation for the cultural heritage of Guimaras. We strive to create an immersive experience that fosters a deeper connection between the audience and the enchanting story that define this unique Philippine gem.
